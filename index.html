Portfolio Projects

Welcome to my data science portfolio. Below you will find two of my key projects demonstrating my skills and expertise in data analysis, modeling, and forecasting.

Effective Reproduction Number of COVID-19 in Bolivia

This project involves the estimation and analysis of the effective reproduction number (R) of COVID-19 in Bolivia. The project includes data collection, cleaning, and application of statistical models to track and forecast the spread of the virus.

ARIMA Forecasting for ARS/USD Exchange Rate

This project demonstrates the use of ARIMA models to forecast the exchange rate between the Argentine Peso (ARS) and the US Dollar (USD). It covers the entire process from data acquisition and preprocessing to model selection, evaluation, and forecasting.
